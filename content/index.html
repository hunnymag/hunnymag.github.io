Types of People You See On The Lakefill

by Hunny & Caroline Brown

On the Lakefill, if it's upwards of 60 degrees, you will always see:

1. A person wearing a bikini, almost in spite of the weather

2. Someone jammin’ on a guitar

3. A dance or theater group practicing

4. Someone slacklining

5. A couple of bros tossing a disc

6. Someone reading on the rocks

7. Old folks gettin’ their jog on

8. A couple making out

9. Someone trying and failing to use the Wi-Fi

You know it's true.
